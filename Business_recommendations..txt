BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================================

RECOMMENDATION 1 — Central Region Discount Optimization
----------------------------------------------------------------
Finding:
Central Region generated $501,239 in sales but recorded the 
lowest profit margin of only 7.92% among all regions.

Root Cause:
Excessive discounting is eroding profit margins in this region.

Recommendation:
Implement a strict discount cap of 15% for Central Region.
Prioritize high-margin products in Central Region sales strategy.

Expected Impact:
Projected profit margin improvement of 5% within 2 quarters.

================================================================

RECOMMENDATION 2 — Furniture Category Restructuring
----------------------------------------------------------------
Finding:
Furniture category generated $741,718 in revenue but achieved 
only 2.55% profit margin — the lowest across all categories.

Root Cause:
High discount rates on furniture products are making this 
category nearly unprofitable.

Recommendation:
Reduce discounts on Furniture sub-categories below 10%.
Focus on premium furniture products with higher margins.
Consider discontinuing low-margin furniture sub-categories.

Expected Impact:
Projected profitability increase of 8% in Furniture category.

================================================================

RECOMMENDATION 3 — Seasonal Sales Strategy
----------------------------------------------------------------
Finding:
Sales consistently peak in September, November and December 
across all 4 years (2015-2018).
January and February recorded lowest sales every year.

Recommendation:
Increase inventory and marketing budget by 20% for Q4 season.
Launch promotional campaigns in January-February to boost Q1.
Introduce "New Year Sale" offers to counter seasonal slowdown.

Expected Impact:
Projected 12% increase in Q1 revenue through targeted promotions.

================================================================

RECOMMENDATION 4 — Home Office Segment Expansion
----------------------------------------------------------------
Finding:
Home Office segment has the highest profit margin at 14.05%
but only 148 customers — significantly lower than Consumer (409)
and Corporate (236) segments.

Recommendation:
Launch targeted marketing campaigns for Home Office customers.
Introduce Home Office specific product bundles.
Offer loyalty programs to retain high-margin HO customers.

Expected Impact:
Expanding Home Office segment by 20% could add significant 
revenue while maintaining highest profit margins.
